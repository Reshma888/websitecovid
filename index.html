"How to stay safe from COVID19"

SYMPTOMS

  • Headache and sore throat
  • Cough
  • Shaking chills
  • Fever
  • Runny nose
  • Dyspnoea

CONTAGION



  • Contaminated objects or substances



  • Air transmission



  • Transmission among humans

PREVENTION



Wash/sanitise your hands regularly




Use nose-rag while coughing and sneezing




Use masks




Avoid contact with animals






Consult a doctor if you develop flu like symptoms




Avoid unnecessary hospital visits and other journey




Avoid touching your nose,eyes and mouth
